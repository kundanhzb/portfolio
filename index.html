About IIT Madras BS Data Science

The IIT Madras BS Degree in Data Science and Applications
is a unique online program designed to provide students with a strong foundation in programming,
statistics, machine learning, and data-driven decision-making. It is India's first online degree
offered by an IIT and is structured to be flexible, allowing students
from diverse backgrounds to enroll.

Key Features:

Flexible Learning:
Study at your own pace with multiple exit options (Foundation, Diploma, or BS Degree).

Industry-Relevant Curriculum:
Covers Python, SQL, Machine Learning, AI, and Big Data Analytics.

Open to All:
No prior coding experience required; anyone with an interest in data science can apply.

High-Quality Faculty:
Learn from IIT Madras professors and industry experts.

Career Support:
Internship and placement assistance with top tech companies.
